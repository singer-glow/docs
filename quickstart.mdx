## Overview

This guide walks you through connecting your first integration to Glow.

## Step 1: Access the Glow Console

Log in to your Glow account at [console.glowsecurity.com](https://console.glowsecurity.com).

<Note>
  Don't have an account? Contact your Glow representative or visit [glowsecurity.com](https://www.glowsecurity.com) to get started.
</Note>

## Step 2: Connect an Integration

Navigate to **Settings → Integrations** and select the tool you want to connect.

<Card title="CrowdStrike Setup" icon="shield-halved" href="/integrations/crowdstrike">
  Follow our step-by-step guide to connect CrowdStrike Falcon.
</Card>

## Step 3: Verify Connection

Once connected, Glow will begin syncing data from your integration. You can verify the connection status in **Settings → Integrations**.

<Check>
  A green status indicator means your integration is connected and syncing.
</Check>
